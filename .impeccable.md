## Design Context

### Users
Internal Astro team — content creators and social media managers at Astro (major Malaysian media company). Daily productivity tool for generating Facebook posts from articles and creating Shopee affiliate content. Professional office context, high-frequency repeated use.

### Brand Personality
**Three words**: Clean, AI, modern
**Brand color**: Black
**Personality**: Precise, capable, focused
**Emotional goal**: Users should feel like they're using a professional, polished tool that respects their intelligence and time — not a generic web app.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Visual tone**: Editorial minimalism with AI-forward precision
- **Primary color**: neutral-950 (#0a0a0a) — sidebar, primary buttons, all active states
- **Background**: Warm off-white (#f7f7f6) with pure white card surfaces
- **Typography**: Inter — clean, legible, preferred by team
- **Radius**: rounded-xl for containers, rounded-lg for controls
- **Borders**: Thin (1px), neutral-200 — no heavy shadows
- **Mode**: Light only
- **Anti-references**: No blue SaaS palette, no glassmorphism, no gradient accents, no rounded-2xl everywhere

### Design Principles
1. **Black is the brand** — neutral-950 replaces all blue; sidebar is black
2. **Typography drives hierarchy** — Space Grotesk, weight variation, tracking-tight on headings
3. **Clean surfaces** — white on warm off-white, thin borders, no shadow-sm everywhere
4. **Semantic colors only** — green = success/done, red = error; nothing decorative
5. **Confident interactions** — strong contrast, zero ambiguity, precise affordances
